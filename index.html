project

revo – waste-to-cash app
concept developer | agile coil social entrepreneurship program 2024

revo is a conceptual mobile app designed to promote sustainable waste management by converting household and business waste into cash rewards. the platform enables users to schedule pickups, sort waste, and earn points based on recyclable value. as the concept developer, i focused on creating a user-friendly, reward-based system integrated with gamification elements and smart city initiatives.

this project was part of the agile coil social entrepreneurship program, where i collaborated with a team to develop and pitch revo as a scalable social impact solution. through this experience, i strengthened my skills in ideation, sustainable design thinking, and solution-oriented innovation.
